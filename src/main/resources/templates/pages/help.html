Frequently Asked Questions

To enable 2FA:

  1. Go to Settings from the navigation menu
  2. Click on Two-Factor Authentication tab
  3. Choose your preferred method (Email or SMS)
  4. Follow the setup instructions to verify your method
  5. Click Enable 2FA to activate protection
We strongly recommend enabling 2FA for enhanced security.

To create a new course:

  1. Navigate to Courses in the main menu
  2. Click the Add New Course button
  3. Fill in the course details (name, description, dates)
  4. Assign a trainer if applicable
  5. Set the course schedule and duration
  6. Click Save Course to create it
Tip: You can always edit course details later from the course management page.

To manage student enrollment:

  1. Go to Students section
  2. Select a student or add a new one
  3. Use the Enroll in Course feature
  4. Choose the appropriate course from the list
  5. Set enrollment date and status
  6. Confirm the enrollment

You can also bulk enroll students using the import feature.

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click Forgot Password? link
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for a reset link
  5. Click the link and set a new password
  6. Log in with your new password
Reset links expire after 24 hours for security reasons.

To upload your profile picture:

  1. Click on your profile name in the top navigation
  2. Select Profile from the dropdown
  3. Click on the camera icon or Change Avatar button
  4. Choose an image file (JPG, PNG, or GIF)
  5. The image will be automatically resized and optimized
  6. Click Upload to save your new avatar
Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF. Maximum file size: 5MB.
